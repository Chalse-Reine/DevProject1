Black Cats are Great

Black cats are seen as a sign of bad luck when they cross someone's path or are associated with darkness. There is some stigma surrounding these lovely guys that is just unnecessary. They are not as bad as we make them out to be and black cats can be a great companion.


The history about Black cats

It started in the European Middle ages really where black cats crossing your path means bad luck will follow. Being seen as a witch's familiar and much more sinister traits, this frindly feline is regarded as the least likely to be adopted. The stigma that black cats are bad juju is a belief that is widespread across many cultures.

Facts about Black cats

  • Black cats are a sailor's best friend
